Mark Zuckerberg

Provide information about Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is an American technology entrepreneur best known as the co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook). He is considered one of the most influential figures in the development of social media worldwide.

Basic Information

Full name: Mark Elliot Zuckerberg
Date of birth: May 14, 1984
Place of birth: White Plains, New York, United States
Nationality: American
Profession: Technology entrepreneur, programmer
Company: Meta Platforms

The Beginning of Facebook

In 2004, while studying at Harvard University, Zuckerberg created a website called Facebook with several of his friends. At first, the platform was only available for Harvard students. Later, it expanded to other universities and eventually to people around the world. Today, Facebook has billions of users and is one of the largest social media platforms globally.

Transition to Meta

In 2021, Zuckerberg changed the company name from Facebook to Meta Platforms to focus more on developing the metaverse, a concept of immersive virtual digital worlds.
